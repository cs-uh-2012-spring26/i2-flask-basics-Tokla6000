# Collection Fields

The following are the minimum fields you need for job seekers and companies. You are free to add more fields now or later, or to create new collections as needed as the project progresses.

## Job Seekers

- First Name (string)
- Last Name (string)
- Email (string in email format. This must be unique; each email corresponds to a unique job seeker profile)
- Expertise (string. Comma-seperated list of keywords describing applicant expertise)
- Years of experience (integer)

## Companies

- Company Name (string)
- Contact Email (string in email format. This must be unique; each email corresponds to a unique job seeker profile)
- Country (string)
- Description (string describing what the company does)
